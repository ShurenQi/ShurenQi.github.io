Biography

I am a first year Ph.D. student under the supervision of Prof. Yushu Zhang at Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA). My current research focuses on invariant feature extraction and visual signal representation for robust pattern recognition and multimedia-content forensics/security applications. My work lies in the mathematical foundations of low-level visual tasks, especially the design of theory and implementation of moment invariants. Such algorithms are generic in their nature and can find their use in numerous application areas.
